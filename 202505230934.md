Distributivity
$$\forall A,B\in\mathbb{R}^{m\times n}, C,D\in\mathbb{R}^{n\times p}$$
$$
\begin{array}{l}
(A+B)\cdot C = AC + BC\\
A\cdot(C+D) = AC+ AD
\end{array}
$$
- Scalar multiplication with $\lambda\in\mathbb{R}$ preserve distributivity $$λ(B+ C) = λB+ λC$$
